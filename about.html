Gyutto Games

"Gyutto" (ぎゅっと) means "tight, compact, bundled" – perfect for quick, fun games on your venue's floor.

Tight, Compact, Bundled Fun for Your Venue

What Is Gyutto Games?

  • Brand name from Japanese "gyutto" meaning tight, compact, bundled
  • A turnkey mobile lobby + mini-game platform customized for your venue
  • Designed for short, engaging rounds with a clear winner

Game Format

  • A modern, interactive, multi-participant evolution of the classic scratch-and-win format
  • Short 2-5 minute rounds, ideal for groups of 2–6 players
  • Players enter a lobby with your venue's branding directly on their mobile phones, no app install required
  • Each round ends with a definitive winner, driving excitement
  • Participating patrons earn venue loyalty points

Token Sales

  • Guests purchase QR tokens (e.g. R 10 for 2 players, 3 rounds or R20 for up to 6 players, 3 rounds) at your counter (POS integration available)
  • Tokens unlock game sessions via unique one-time, venue-locked codes
  • Easy tracking: your dashboard shows tokens sold, activated, and redeemed

Brand & Menu Integration

  • Lobby & game skins match your venue's colors, logo, and menu items
  • Prizes tied to menu (e.g. free appetizer, drink discount, loyalty points)
  • Customizable prize tiers and game odds to fit your cost structure

Engage Waiting Guests

  • Patrons play while waiting for meals/drinks—reduces perceived wait time
  • Patrons are incentivised to play due to chance of winning discount on ordered meal
  • Increases dwell time, leading to additional orders (drinks, sides, desserts)
  • Keeps tables active and staff busy in a positive way

Dual Revenue Streams

  • Token Sales Profit: you retain the full ticket price on each token sold
  • Sales Uplift: longer stays and additional orders translate to higher average checks

Optional In-Venue Enhancements

  • TV or projector leaderboards to showcase top scores in real time
  • Social share stations: capture gameplay clips for instant Instagram/TikTok posts
  • Branded collateral: table tents, counter displays, scratch-off covers for tokens

How to Get Started

  • Sign Up: 4-week setup including white-labeling and initial token batch
  • Launch: tokens on sale—guests scan and play immediately
  • Optimize: use your dashboard to adjust prize tiers, monitor sales, and upsell enhancements
  • Contact: reach out for a free demo and custom pricing proposal
