What Is Gyutto Games?

  • A subscription-based turnkey mobile gaming platform fully customized to your venue's brand and menu
  • Customers purchase game tokens from your establishment, you keep 100% of token sales revenue
  • Short, engaging multiplayer games with instant winners and real-time social sharing capabilities
  • Flexible participation: guests can play anonymously or log in (registered players earn venue loyalty points with every game)

How Games Work

  • 5-minute game sessions with 3-5 rounds, perfect for groups of 2–6 players waiting for food or drinks
  • Players purchase game tokens (unique codes) to access your branded game lobby instantly – no app downloads required
  • Game Winners stand a (randomized) chance to become Prize Winners
  • Prize Winners receive immediate prize notifications for discounts, menu items, or loyalty rewards
  • You maintain complete control over prize types, values, and win frequencies

Token-Based Revenue Model

  • Token bundles included in monthly subscription, top-up via your dashboard at any time
  • Guests purchase game tokens at your counter (pricing customizable to your market)
  • Each token generates a unique access code for one game session per venue
  • Real-time analytics: track tokens sold, activated, and prizes redeemed through your dashboard

Complete Brand Integration

  • Prizes directly tied to your menu (e.g., appetizer upgrades, drink specials, percentage discounts)
  • Flexible prize configuration lets you adjust rewards and odds to match your margins
