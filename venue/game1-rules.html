üé≤ How to Play

1 Initial Deal: Everyone gets 2 cards face up. The dealer gets 1 face up, 1 face down.
2 Your Turn: Choose to HIT (take another card) or STAND (keep your current total).
3 Other Players: Watch as Player 2 and Player 3 make their decisions automatically.
4 Dealer's Turn: The dealer reveals their hidden card and hits until they reach 12 or higher.
5 Scoring: Whoever is closest to 15 without going over wins the round!

üéØ Game Structure

Tournament Format

Each game consists of 3 rounds. Win the most rounds to become the champion!

Dealer Rules

The dealer must hit (take cards) until their total is 12 or higher, then they must stand.

Strategy Tip: Since the target is only 15, you'll usually need 3+ cards to get close. Don't be afraid to hit on lower totals like 8 or 9!
